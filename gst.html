GST Registration

Businesses and organisations carrying out taxable activities may be required to register for GST where annual taxable turnover exceeds the registration threshold prescribed by law.

Registered persons must charge GST on taxable supplies, retain proper financial records, and file GST returns for each filing period.

Common Registration Requirements

  • Carrying on a taxable activity
  • Meeting the GST turnover threshold
  • Holding a valid revenue number
  • Maintaining accurate financial records
